### Card Meaning

Upheaval reveals truth. What feels like collapse is actually exposure—systems, beliefs, relationships that were never as solid as you thought. This is not punishment. This is clarity arriving with force. Where in your life have you been building on shaky ground? What illusion is being shattered so you can see what's real?

The Tower comes when something must fall so something truer can rise. Notice where you're clinging to structures—internal or external—that no longer serve you. What are you defending that's already crumbling? Sometimes the kindest thing the universe can do is let the false foundation break so you can finally build on truth.

## Key Design Elements

### Information Hierarchy

1. **Card Image** - Visual anchor
2. **Card Meaning** - Static, therapeutic interpretation (your voice)
3. **Active Insight** - Dynamic, contextual wisdom (also your voice, but responsive to their moment)
4. **Optional Elements**:
   - Transit timeline (for visual context)
   - Reflection history (if repeat draw)
   - Journal prompt (contextual)

### Visual Distinction

The **Active Insight** should be visually distinct (maybe a subtle background tint, different typography, or an icon) to signal: *"This is personalized for you, right now, based on where you are in your journey."*

### Tone Consistency

Both sections maintain your emotionally intelligent, question-based approach. The active insight doesn't contradict the card meaning—it **extends** it with astrological context.

### Progressive Disclosure

Users can read just the card meaning and get value. The active insight is **bonus depth** for those who want to understand why this card appeared *today*, during *this* transit, for the *nth* time.
